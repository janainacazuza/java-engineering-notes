# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

This project is a space for technical learning. All interaction must be respectful, direct, and content-oriented.

## Expected behavior

- Clear and objective technical communication.
- Respect for disagreement on content.
- Acceptance of contribution refusal without hostility.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Offensive language or imagery.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Harassment of any nature.
- Publication of third parties' private information.

## Enforcement

Reports may be sent to the maintainer via email or private issue. Every report is reviewed. The maintainer has authority to remove comments, close issues, and ban participants who violate this code.

This code of conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
